About

Multidisciplinary artist working at the intersection of live coding, audiovisual performance, graphic notation, and sound. Practice rooted in SuperCollider and TidalCycles, exploring algorithmic composition and real-time sound synthesis. Background in aerospace R&D and architecture informs a technical approach to artistic creation, with particular interest in translating spatial and structural thinking into graphic scores and sonic form.
