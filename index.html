About

Her practice is rooted in the intersection of algorithmic systems and gestural intuition. She creates live-coded electronic music in SuperCollider, where composition emerges through written code in real-time. This computational approach coexists with tactile, hand-driven processes — charcoal and pencil drawings, 3D sculptural work. Currently, she is developing engineering projects informed by her R&D experience in aerospace within the military sector, exploring how technical knowledge translates into artistic inquiry.
